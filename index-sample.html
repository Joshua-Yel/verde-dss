How staffing recommendations are derived: Forecasted sessions per day are compared against the overall average. Days that are 15% above average are flagged as High Demand (recommend full staff + 1 on-call), days within ±15% are Normal (standard staffing), and days 15% below average are Low Demand (minimum staffing sufficient).

Seasonal Peak Note

Based on the salon's records, December shows revenue of approximately ₱1,499,850 — about 13× the average of the other recorded months (~₱114,400), reflecting an extreme holiday season surge. Salon management should plan for significantly increased staffing, bulk inventory orders, and extended hours leading into December to capture this seasonal demand and avoid the stockouts seen in the Dec–Feb period.

About This Prototype

How this system fits into the thesis "Developing an AI-Assisted Demand Forecasting System for a Beauty Salon."

What This Prototype Demonstrates

This is a working prototype built using Puroy Salon's actual 2025 operational records (November 2024 – May 2025). It demonstrates the core functionality envisioned for the full system: turning raw operational data into forward-looking forecasts across four key areas — service demand, inventory, finances, and staffing.

Data Entry & Management Data Cleaning & Validation Analytics & Forecasting Dashboards & Reports

Alignment with the IPO Model

INPUTService records, inventory data, overhead costs, salaries, operational logs
PROCESSData entry → cleaning → analytics & forecasting (this prototype)
OUTPUTDemand, Inventory, Financial, Staffing forecasts + Dashboards (4 tabs)

Forecasting Method Used

The default forecasting method is a Weighted Moving Average (WMA), which gives more importance to recent months while still considering older data. This is a transparent, explainable statistical method — appropriate for a salon's relatively small, monthly-aggregated dataset, and easy for non-technical salon owners and employees to understand and trust (per the Technology Acceptance Model and Information System Success Model in the study's theoretical framework).

In the full system, this layer can be extended with more advanced AI/ML models (e.g., regression, ensemble methods) as more historical data becomes available — consistent with the study's "AI-assisted, machine-learning-supported statistical forecasting" framing.

Data Source

All figures in this prototype are drawn from Puroy Salon's recorded operational data: a Daily Operations Log (Jan 1 – May 29, 2025, 149 days), a Revenue by Service tracker (Nov 2024 – Apr 2025, 12 services), Monthly Expenses, Net Income Summary, and a Chemical Inventory Tracker (7 products, Nov 2024 – Apr 2025).
